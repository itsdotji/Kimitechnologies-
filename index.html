Our purpose

Our Mission

Why we started Kimi Technologies and what drives us every day.

Kimi Technologies was founded in 2023 as an independent AI and robotics media brand. The idea came from a simple observation: most coverage of artificial intelligence and robotics is either too superficial or too technical for anyone outside a research lab to understand. We wanted to build something different.

Our core purpose is to make AI and robotics developments understandable for professionals and enthusiasts without oversimplifying the substance or hiding behind jargon. We believe that the people building the future deserve clear, honest, and practical information about the tools and systems they work with.

"We combine hands-on frontend development experience with content and analysis. We build the technology as well as write about it. This gives us a perspective that is grounded, practical, and rooted in real-world use."

What makes us different is exactly this combination. We are developers who write and writers who build. When we talk about web development, SEO, or content strategy, we're speaking from direct experience. When we cover AI and robotics, we've tested the tools, read the papers, and built systems ourselves.

Our long term vision is to become a sharp, trusted, independent voice covering the intersection of AI, robotics, and digital strategy. We want to be the place where professionals go to understand what's real, what's coming, and what it means for their work.

We're always open to collaboration. If you're working in AI, robotics, tech media, or digital strategy and you share our values, we'd love to hear from you. Get in touch through our contact page.

Good SEO matters because it helps the right people find the right content. We build strategies that are sustainable, ethical, and aligned with how search engines actually work. No shortcuts, no black hat tactics, just solid, well structured optimization.

OPENQR

MIT License Client side Privacy focused

OPENQR is a client side QR code generator with broad customization options. It allows users to generate QR codes for URLs, text, contact information, and more, all without uploading any data to a server. Everything runs in the browser.

What makes OPENQR useful is its privacy-first approach. No data is sent to any server. No tracking. No analytics. Just a fast, reliable QR code generator that you can use with complete confidence. It's released under the MIT License, so you can use it, modify it, and build on it freely.

  • Fully client side, no server uploads
  • Broad customization: colors, size, shape, and more
  • Privacy focused, no tracking or analytics
  • MIT License, free to use and modify
Visit Project

NOT ACCESS

Password protected Decentralized Offline PWA

NOT ACCESS is a client side, password protected, decentralized, offline Progressive Web Application. It solves the problem of secure, offline-first access without relying on external servers or third party authentication.

The application is built to work entirely in the browser. Data is stored locally, passwords are verified client side, and the app works offline. This makes it ideal for situations where you need secure access to information without an internet connection or without trusting a central server.

  • Client side password protection, no server required
  • Decentralized, no single point of failure
  • Offline first Progressive Web Application
  • Secure and private by design
Visit Project

CLIPO

Browser based AI Background remover No cloud uploads

CLIPO is an open source background remover and editor powered by browser based AI. It allows users to remove image backgrounds, add custom backgrounds, and export high quality results without uploading files to external servers. Everything runs locally in the browser using machine learning models that are loaded once and run on your device.

This matters for privacy and speed. Your images never leave your device. There are no cloud uploads, no data storage, and no hidden processing fees. You get fast, private, high quality background removal with full control over the result.

  • Browser based AI, no server processing
  • Privacy first: images never leave your device
  • Add custom backgrounds and export high quality results
  • Open source, free to use and modify
